B2 Grammar: Could, Would and Passive Voice

This section practises language for explaining how a future payment system might work.

UsePatternExample
Future possibilitycould + verbThe digital euro could become a reality.
Hypothetical resultwould + verbYour friend would receive the money instantly.
Passive processbe + past participlePayments are made instantly.
Same valuewill always beA euro will always be a euro.
